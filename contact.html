FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a stay at Mist Mountain Forest Gate?

The easiest way is to WhatsApp us at +91 82619 22889 or fill out the contact form above. We'll confirm availability and share pricing within a few hours.

Is Mist Mountain Forest Gate suitable for families with children?

Absolutely! We're a family-friendly resort with safe surroundings, child-friendly activities like nature walks, and spacious family villa accommodation.

What is the best time to visit?

October to March is ideal for pleasant weather and clear skies. Monsoon (June–September) offers lush greenery and waterfalls, but roads can be slippery.

Is food included in the stay?

Yes, all stays include home-cooked Maharashtra-style breakfast and dinner. Additional meals and snacks can be arranged on request.

How far is Mist Mountain from Nashik city?

We are approximately 1 hour from Nashik city, 20 minutes from Trimbakeshwar, and 3.5 hours from Mumbai via Kasara/Igatpuri route.

Do you accept group / corporate bookings?

Yes! We accommodate groups for team outings, family reunions, and small celebrations. Contact us for special group rates and custom packages.
