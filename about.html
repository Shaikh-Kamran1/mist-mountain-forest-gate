About Mist Mountain
Forest Gate

The Beginning

Born From a Love
of Forests & Nature

Mist Mountain Forest Gate was created with a simple dream — to offer a genuine, peaceful escape from the chaotic urban life. Nestled in the rich green hills of Ghoti, near the sacred town of Trimbakeshwar, our resort brings you face-to-face with raw, untouched nature.

The founders, passionate nature lovers from Maharashtra, wanted to create a space where families could reconnect, couples could find romance in the mist, and solo travelers could rediscover themselves among the trees.

What started as a small forest cottage has grown into a cherished weekend destination — yet we've never lost our soul: simplicity, warmth, and an unbreakable bond with nature.

Our Surroundings

The Nature That
Surrounds You

Every direction you look at Mist Mountain Forest Gate reveals breathtaking natural beauty.

Dense Forest Canopy

Ancient trees form a natural canopy overhead, filling the air with pure oxygen and the sweet fragrance of forest earth. Perfect for morning meditations and evening walks.

Seasonal Waterfalls

During monsoon and early winter, cascading waterfalls grace the hillsides nearby. The sound of falling water is nature's own lullaby — utterly therapeutic.

Misty Sunrise Peaks

Wake early to witness the golden sun rising over misty mountain ridges. The Sahyadri peaks create a stunning silhouette that will stay in your memory forever.

Rich Wildlife & Birds

Over 60 bird species have been spotted in and around our resort. From peacocks to hornbills, nature's orchestra wakes you every morning.
