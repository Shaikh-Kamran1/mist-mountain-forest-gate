Our Story

A Hidden Gem in
Maharashtra's Forest Hills

Tucked away in the lush greenery of Ghoti near Trimbakeshwar, Mist Mountain Forest Gate is a nature escape designed for those who crave quietude, fresh mountain air, and an unfiltered connection with nature.

Just a few hours from Mumbai, Nashik & Pune — perfect for weekend getaways, family vacations, couple retreats, and small group celebrations surrounded by forests, birdsong, and breathtaking views.

🌿

Untouched Nature

Surrounded by dense forest trails, seasonal waterfalls, and fresh mountain air.

🔥

Bonfire & Outdoor Dining

Enjoy evening bonfires under the stars and freshly cooked local meals.

📍

Prime Location

Near Trimbakeshwar temple, Anjaneri fort, and scenic Ghoti region of Nashik.
